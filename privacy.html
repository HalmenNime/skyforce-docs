Privacy Policy

LAST UPDATED: MAY 2026  |  VERSION 1.0

Overview

SkyForce is a Discord-based aviation RPG bot developed by Halmen. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and how it is used.

By using SkyForce, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this document.

Data We Collect

SkyForce collects only the minimum data necessary to operate the game:

  • Discord User ID — to identify your account in our database
  • Game progress — XP, level, balance, equipped items
  • Unlocked assets — jets and rockets you have obtained
  • Discord locale — used to display the bot in your preferred language

We do not collect: usernames, email addresses, messages, or any personal information beyond what is listed above.

How Data Is Used

Collected data is used exclusively to:

  • Store and retrieve your in-game progress
  • Display your profile and statistics
  • Provide localized responses based on your language settings

Your data is never sold, shared, or transferred to third parties.

Data Storage & Retention

Data is stored locally on the bot's host server. We retain your data for as long as you use the bot.

You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us. Upon request, all data associated with your Discord User ID will be permanently deleted within 7 days.

Local Storage No Cloud Sharing Deletion on Request

Your Rights

  • Request a copy of all data stored about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request complete deletion of your data — note: deletion is permanent and irreversible
  • Opt out at any time by stopping use of the bot

In accordance with GDPR, users have the right to access, correct, and delete their personal data by contacting skyforce.rpg@gmail.com.
